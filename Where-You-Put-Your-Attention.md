---
title: 'Where You Put Your Attention'
date: '2026-03-05'
category: 'thought'
excerpt: 'Attention is the most honest form of love.'
---

Whatever you give your attention to — that is what you are choosing.

Not what you say you value. Not what you tell people matters to you. But where your eyes go when nothing is forcing them. What you think about in the quiet moments before sleep. What you return to without being asked.

Attention is the most honest thing about a person.

Pay attention to where yours goes today. It will tell you something true.
